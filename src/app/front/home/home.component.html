At DPS RN training in visual art is not limited to applying colours or sketching on a surface but it works at many levels to develop aesthetics, appreciation and constructing a visual grammar. Right from pre primary students are trained to observe without loosing perspective and some of these are core scientific skills. Hence art even delineates the spectrum of scientific attitude
Children have a world of their own. Here at school we provide them a canvas to etch out their feelings which cascade out into beautiful stories. The collection of these outpourings is OFF THE BEATEN PATH
"Wow! Beyond imagination" , said one parent who saw her ward , presenting his research on the stage. Captivating performances which will compel you to applaud.
Junoon is a bouquet of visual and performing art activities encompassing a week which culminat into candid expressions of students where they create the whole show on the last day. Brainchild of actor Sanjana Kapoor daughter of legendary Shashi Kapoor, it brings in artists from all across India to interact with students.
DPS RAJNAGAR has a state of the art sports infrastructure. Half Olympic size covered Swimming pool prepares children for this important life skill. Eight layered special synthetic surface two Basket Ball and two Tennis court prevents knee and leg injuries and provide ample space for practice. School has soccer court much loved by the children and two cemented cricket pitches for all weather practice. Indoor skating , Badminton, TT , carrom and Chess are the other highlights of the school.
Students try their hands at Volleyball and practice in Long Jump pits for building leg muscle strength along with developing prowess in the said event. We have cemented circles for throwing Shot Put and Discuss. Students have won accolades and have honed their skills.
Co-curricular activities are interwoven along with Scholastic studies, so that students are able to assimilate the nuances of diverse skills in the dynamic Educational space through various forums. Summer camp, Pratibha ki udaan, assemblies, celebration of thematic weeks and so on give children a chance to express and excel.
